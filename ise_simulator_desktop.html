The Institute of Sonic Epistemologies

Online Simulator

This is an online simulator for a binaural sound installation by Ludwig Zeller and Martin Rumori. It has been originally developed for use on a Raspberry Pi with a touchscreen of 480x320 pixels. This is a slightly adapted desktop version. Javascript support required.

Please find further information on the background of the installation and its technical realisation at https://git.iem.at/rumori/The_Institute_of_Sonic_Epistemologies.
